Preface

I've only started reading recently. It started in the summer of 2024 with a little book called The Perfect Predator.
I thought that book was *okay*, it was interesting in the beginning, but towards the end, it became a bowl of brain molasses.
So in the times since, I've been reading a lot of philosophy, a genre that I am endlessly interested in. I started with, and am still reading/rereading Either/Or
by Soren Kierkegaard.
